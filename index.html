About the Ebook

"Motherhood is beautiful but it can also be overwhelming, stretching you in ways you never imagined"

"Mom’s Calm Code" is not just another list of rules or rigid routines. it's a book of heart-deep truth, honest reflections, and practical "codes" to help you understand your emotions, respond instead of reach, and rediscover the woman behind the role. Through storytelling, scripture, and real-life experiences. You will learn how to stay grounded in 'grace' not in 'guilt'". This is a blend of resilience, balance, and wisdom gained, written with the nurturing voice of a mother. Get ready to be inspired and equipped to face both nature and life with grace.

"This book is your gentle companion no matter the phase you are in, expecting your first child, raising the little ones as a mother, or a woman preparing her spirit for that future in-view. This book is the compass to lead you graciously in a divine way."
